Welcome to Switzerland

About Switzerland Tourism

Over the past few years, the Switzerland tourism industry has grown to a great extent due to the well-developed network of transportation, and the efficient maintenance of all the tourist spots in Switzerland. Another main reason for the growth in Switzerland tourism is that the place can be visited at any time of the year. Switzerland also serves as a perfect romantic getaway for couples, with its beautiful mountains and lakes. One must have a Switzerland trip guide and a tourist map of Switzerland in order to explore some of the best areas of Switzerland.

Places to visit in Switzerland:

Being a famous tourist destination, there are many places and cities to visit in Switzerland. Zurich and Bern are the top places to visit in Switzerland in summer. They offer some of the best views of the place, with beautiful lake and Gothic architecture. These two cities are the best cities to visit in Switzerland as they give an idea of the culture and lifestyle of Switzerland as a whole. Apart from this, there are many things to do in Switzerland like visiting the Rhine Falls which gives a spectacular view of the water body when seen from one of the viewing platforms. The Rhine Falls are one of the best places to visit in July that is during the summer season because there are chances of getting wet in the falls.

Culture in Switzerland:

Most of the Swiss people are fluent in more than one language. Being known as the land of four languages, Swiss and German are widely spoken throughout the country among other European languages. Influenced with Alpine tradition, Swiss cuisine has affinity with Italian and German cuisine. The country is especially known for its chocolate, milk, and cheese-based dishes like fondue and raclette. Its cuisines depict true celebration of gastronomy while gooey cheese served along with velvety chocolate.
